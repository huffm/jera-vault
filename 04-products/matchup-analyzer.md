# Matchup Analyzer

## Product Positioning

The Matchup Analyzer is a product lab prototype that applies structured analysis workflow design to sports matchup review.

It should be presented as a focused exploration of matchup context, market signals, structured reasoning, and compact decision support briefs.

## What It Does

The matchup analyzer organizes matchup information into a structured review workflow. It helps gather signals, compare relevant factors, and produce outputs that can be reviewed.

## Product Focus

The product should focus on:

- Structured analysis.
- Signal gathering.
- Reviewable outputs.
- Compact decision support briefs.
- Productized AI workflow design.

## What It Demonstrates

The matchup analyzer demonstrates that Jera Technologies can:

- Apply a reusable AI workflow direction to a specific domain.
- Turn complex domain analysis into a usable product.
- Design interfaces around structured decision support.
- Build a product experience that makes analysis easier to inspect.

## Boundaries

Do not frame the product as a betting prediction engine. Do not promise picks, profits, betting outcomes, or model accuracy. The product is a decision support application, not a guarantee system.
