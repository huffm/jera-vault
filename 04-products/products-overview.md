# Products Overview

## Product Portfolio Positioning

Jera Technologies maintains a product portfolio of Jera built products that demonstrate practical software engineering, applied AI workflow design, automation, and decision support.

The products are owned products and product examples. They should not be framed as client projects unless explicitly provided later.

## Current Products

### DAI Internal Product Direction

DAI remains the internal product direction for structured AI workflows.

For the public site, do not lead with the DAI name. Use business facing terms such as Structured Analysis Pipeline, Decision Artifact Pipeline, Analysis Workflow Platform, Reviewable Analysis System, or Structured Decision Support Workflow.

The current public page uses Structured Analysis Pipeline.

### Matchup Analyzer

The Matchup Analyzer is a Jera built decision support application that applies structured analysis workflow design to sports matchup review. It focuses on structured analysis, signal gathering, reviewable outputs, and clear decision artifacts.

## Product Portfolio Role

The product portfolio should show what Jera Technologies can build through its engineering services:

- AI enabled workflows.
- Structured analysis pipelines.
- Decision support applications.
- Productized technical ideas.
- Usable interfaces around complex logic.

## Public Copy Boundaries

- Do not describe products as casual work.
- Do not imply paid client delivery without proof.
- Do not frame the matchup analyzer as a betting prediction engine.
- Do not guarantee model accuracy, profits, or outcomes.
