# 0003 Use Luminous Technical Visual System

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The Jera Technologies public website needs to feel more polished than a normal portfolio while staying more grounded than a loud creative agency site.

The brand should feel calm, senior, precise, technical, product minded, modern, trustworthy, and quietly premium.

## Decision

Use a premium hybrid visual system called Luminous Technical.

## Rationale

Luminous Technical combines a warm readable base with deep technical panels, soft blue and cyan accents, glass cards, rounded containers, crisp typography, strong whitespace, and subtle motion.

This supports business clarity and product confidence without making the site feel like a generic SaaS template or a flashy agency site.

## Consequences

- The site avoids both a plain light theme and a full dark theme.
- Product and CTA sections can use deep navy panels for contrast.
- Public pages can feel serious and polished while staying readable.
- Motion remains minimal and nonessential.

## Slice 1 Result

The initial Astro site implements Luminous Technical in `src/styles/global.css`, page layouts, hero composition, cards, product panels, and CTA sections.
