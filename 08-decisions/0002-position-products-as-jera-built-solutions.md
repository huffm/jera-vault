# 0002 Position Products As Jera Built Solutions

## Status

Accepted

## Context

DAI and the matchup analyzer need to support the Jera Technologies website without being framed as casual work, disconnected portfolio pieces, or unverified client projects.

## Decision

Position DAI and the matchup analyzer as Jera built products, owned products, and solution examples within the Jera Technologies product portfolio.

## Rationale

This framing is accurate, credible, and useful for the public website. It shows what Jera Technologies can build through its engineering services without implying paid client work.

## Consequences

- Product pages can be formal and business facing.
- The products can demonstrate applied AI software, structured analysis pipelines, and decision support applications.
- Solution examples can reference the products as engineering patterns.
- Public copy must avoid guarantees, gambling centered framing, unprovided client claims, and private product details.

## Current Website Result

The current Products page and product detail pages position the Structured Analysis Pipeline and the Matchup Analyzer as Jera built products in the Jera Technologies product portfolio.

DAI remains the internal product direction, but public copy now uses the business facing Structured Analysis Pipeline name.
