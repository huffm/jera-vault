# Structured Decision Support System

## Summary

A structured decision support system turns source material, analysis, evaluation, and synthesis into clear outputs that people can review.

This solution example aligns with the DAI product direction and shows how Jera Technologies can build applied AI software around human judgment.

## Problem

Many AI assisted workflows produce answers without enough structure. Teams need outputs that show what was considered, how signals were organized, and what should be reviewed before a decision is made.

## Approach

Jera Technologies would design a workflow that:

- Collects relevant source material.
- Organizes inputs into a structured analysis pipeline.
- Evaluates competing signals.
- Produces a reviewable decision support output.
- Keeps human review visible in the workflow.

## Resulting Software

The resulting software could be a web application, internal tool, or product workflow that helps teams move from raw information to structured analysis and clear recommendations.

## What This Demonstrates

This example demonstrates Applied AI, AI assisted review workflows, decision support, and structured output design.
