# AI Decision Artifact System

## Summary

An AI decision artifact system turns research, retrieval, analysis, and synthesis into structured outputs that people can review.

This solution example aligns with the DAI product direction and shows how Jera Technologies can build applied AI software around human review.

## Problem

Many AI assisted workflows produce answers without enough structure. Teams need outputs that show what was considered, how signals were organized, and what should be reviewed before a decision is made.

## Approach

Jera Technologies would design a workflow that:

- Collects relevant source material.
- Organizes inputs into a structured analysis pipeline.
- Evaluates competing signals.
- Produces a reviewable decision artifact.
- Keeps human review visible in the workflow.

## Resulting Software

The resulting software could be a web application, internal tool, or product workflow that helps teams move from raw information to structured analysis.

## What This Demonstrates

This example demonstrates Applied AI, productized AI workflow design, decision support, and structured output design.
