# Applied AI

## Service Summary

Jera Technologies designs and builds AI enabled applications that support structured workflows, analysis, retrieval, evaluation, and synthesis.

The focus is practical software. AI should improve the workflow, not become the whole message.

## What This Includes

- AI assisted research and analysis workflows.
- Retrieval based product experiences.
- Reviewable decision artifacts.
- Human review and evaluation flows.
- Prompted workflows wrapped in usable application interfaces.
- Structured outputs that can be inspected and improved.

## Business Problems It Supports

- Teams need faster synthesis from scattered information.
- Complex decisions need clearer review artifacts.
- Existing workflows need AI support without losing human judgment.
- Technical ideas need to become polished product experiences.

## Product Examples

The Structured Analysis Pipeline demonstrates this service area through structured analysis, signal organization, reviewable outputs, and human review.

The Matchup Analyzer demonstrates how the same product direction can support structured domain analysis and reviewable outputs.

## Public Copy Direction

Use grounded phrases such as structured AI workflows, reviewable outputs, applied AI software, and decision support. Avoid claims that imply autonomous replacement of expert judgment.
